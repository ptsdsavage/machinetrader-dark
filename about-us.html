Our Story

Meet the Team

MachineTrader™ is the world's only true low-code/no-code trading platform — everything, including the app itself, is customizable without writing a line of Python or C++.

Building Trading Algorithms 10x Faster

MachineTrader's founders have written millions of lines of code over the years, but we find that using our visual platform we can build trading algorithms up to 10x faster than writing code. Our mission is to democratize algorithmic trading by making it accessible to serious traders who want to automate their strategies without needing full-stack programming skills.

Our Team

Dan Savage

CEO & Co-Chairman

Dan is currently the CEO and Co-Chairman of MachineTrader.io Inc. Prior to MachineTrader, Dan served as CEO of Predictive Technology Systems which built the MachineTrader platform. In 2018, Dan co-founded Resolute Digital, a ground-breaking digital marketing agency that had 50 employees and $30MM in annual revenue at the time of its sale to Weber-Shandwick. Earlier in his career, he led the team that developed the first automated system for buying Google AdWords and was a pioneer of using technology to purchase online advertising. Dan also has deep experience in digital strategy and digital marketing and his technical skills include more than fifteen years of writing LAMP stack programs including PHP, MySQL, BigQuery, PERL, and Python. Dan is a lifelong entrepreneur and classically trained musician with a track record of starting and growing successful companies and building outstanding teams.

Entrepreneurship Digital Marketing LAMP Stack AdTech Pioneer
